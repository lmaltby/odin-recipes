Swedish Meatballs

Description

This is a vegetarian take on taco stuffed shells, because my girlfriend and most of her family are vegetarians. Speaking as a non-vegetarian however, these are delicious. So, don't be dissuaded due to meat substitutes, and enjoy a fantastic meal!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of veggie beef crumbles
  • 1 1/2 cups chunky salsa
  • 1 cup of Mexican blend shredded cheese
  • 1 box of jumbo shells
  • 12oz of sour cream
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 cans of El Paso enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cook pasta shells according to their box
  3. Cook veggie beef in a skillet alongside shells
  4. Add taco seasoning and water
  5. Stir and cook until water has disappeared
  6. Stir in salsa and cheese
  7. Scoop mixture into the shells
  8. Place shells in baking dish
  9. Add enchilada sauce
  10. Top with remaining salsa and cheese
  11. Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes
  12. (Optional) Serve with sour cream
